Beginner Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Jumping Jacks – 1 minute
  • Arm Circles – 30 seconds forward, 30 seconds backward
  • High Knees – 1 minute
  • Bodyweight Squats – 10 reps
  • Dynamic Lunges – 5 reps per leg

Workout (30-40 minutes)

Full-Body Circuit (Repeat 2-3 times)

  • Bodyweight Squats – 12-15 reps
  • Incline Push-Ups – 8-10 reps
  • Glute Bridges – 12-15 reps
  • Plank (Knees or Full) – Hold for 20-30 seconds
  • Step-Ups – 8-10 reps per leg
  • Bent-Over Rows (with light weights or bottles) – 10-12 reps

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Hamstring Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds per leg
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 slow reps
  • Child’s Pose – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Shoulder Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds per side
  • Quad Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds per leg

Tips

  • Rest for 30-60 seconds between exercises and 1-2 minutes between circuits.
  • Focus on proper form rather than speed.
  • Hydrate before, during, and after your workout.
  • Gradually increase the number of sets or reps as you gain strength.
