Advanced Level

Warm-Up (10 minutes)

  • Dynamic Stretching (Leg Swings, Arm Circles) – 2 minutes
  • High Knees – 1 minute
  • ***** Kicks – 1 minute
  • Jump Squats – 10 reps
  • Burpees – 10 reps

Workout (40-50 minutes)

Strength and Conditioning Circuit (Repeat 3-4 times)

  • Deadlifts (Barbell) – 8-10 reps
  • Pull-Ups (Weighted if possible) – 8-12 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 8-10 reps per leg
  • Push Press (Barbell or Dumbbell) – 10 reps
  • Kettlebell Swings – 12-15 reps
  • Toes-to-Bar – 8-12 reps

Cool-Down and Stretching (10 minutes)

  • Hamstring Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds per leg
  • Pigeon Pose – Hold for 30 seconds per side
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 slow reps
  • Child’s Pose – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Shoulder Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds per side

Tips

  • Use progressive overload by increasing weight or reps each week.
  • Maintain strict form to avoid injuries.
  • Incorporate active recovery days with yoga or light cardio.
  • Track your progress for motivation and goal setting.
