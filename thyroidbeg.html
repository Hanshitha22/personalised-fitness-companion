Personalized Fitness Companion - Thyroid Exercises

Beginner Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Neck Rolls – 5 reps clockwise and counterclockwise
  • Shoulder Rolls – 10 reps forward and backward
  • Arm Circles – 10 small circles forward and backward
  • Marching in Place – 1 minute

Workout (20-30 minutes)

Thyroid-Friendly Circuit (Repeat 2 times)

  • Standing Forward Bend – Hold for 20 seconds
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 slow reps
  • Chair Pose – Hold for 15-20 seconds
  • Breathing Exercise (Diaphragmatic Breathing) – 1 minute
  • Bridge Pose – Hold for 15-20 seconds

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Child’s Pose – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Cobra Pose – Hold for 20 seconds
  • Seated Forward Bend – Hold for 20 seconds
  • Side Stretches – Hold for 10 seconds per side

Tips

  • Focus on low-impact exercises to reduce fatigue.
  • Maintain proper posture during exercises to avoid strain.
  • Incorporate breathing exercises to relax the mind and body.
  • Stay hydrated and follow a thyroid-friendly diet.
