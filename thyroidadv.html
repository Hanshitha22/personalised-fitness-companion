Personalized Fitness Companion - Thyroid Exercises (Advanced Level)

Warm-Up (10-15 minutes)

  • Dynamic Neck Rolls – 12 reps clockwise and counterclockwise
  • Overhead Arm Circles – 20 reps forward and backward
  • Lateral Lunges – 10 reps per side
  • Jumping Jacks – 2 minutes

Workout (30-40 minutes)

Advanced Thyroid Circuit (Repeat 3-4 times)

  • Warrior Pose III – Hold for 30 seconds per side
  • Side Plank with Hip Dips – 12 dips per side
  • Chair Pose with Arm Pulses – Hold for 20 seconds and pulse for 10 reps
  • Breathing Exercise (Kapalabhati Breathing) – 2-3 minutes
  • Bridge Pose with Weighted Leg Lifts – 12 reps alternating legs

Cool-Down and Stretching (10-15 minutes)

  • Pigeon Pose – Hold for 1 minute per side
  • Upward Facing Dog – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Seated Spinal Twist – Hold for 30 seconds per side
  • Reclined Butterfly Pose – Hold for 1-2 minutes

Tips

  • Engage core muscles in every exercise to maintain balance and posture.
  • Integrate resistance bands or light weights for added intensity.
  • Maintain focus on breath control during advanced poses.
  • Hydrate well and take breaks between circuits if needed.
  • Consistency is key to improving thyroid function and overall fitness.
