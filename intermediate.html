Intermediate Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Jump Rope – 1 minute
  • Arm Swings – 1 minute
  • High Knees – 1 minute
  • Leg Swings – 10 reps per leg

Workout (30-40 minutes)

Strength and Cardio Circuit (Repeat 2-3 times)

  • Push-Ups – 12-15 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 10-12 reps per leg
  • Burpees – 8-10 reps
  • Plank to Shoulder Tap – 10-12 reps
  • Mountain Climbers – 30 seconds
  • Russian Twists (with or without weight) – 15 reps per side

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Standing Forward Fold – Hold for 20 seconds
  • Pigeon Pose – Hold for 20 seconds per leg
  • Downward Dog – Hold for 20-30 seconds
  • Seated Spinal Twist – Hold for 20 seconds per side

Tips

  • Focus on controlled movements and proper form.
  • Take short breaks (15-30 seconds) between exercises if needed.
  • Keep your core engaged during all exercises.
  • Hydrate well and listen to your body.
