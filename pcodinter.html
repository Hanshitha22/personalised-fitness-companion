Intermediate Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Dynamic Lunges – 10 reps per leg
  • Arm Circles – 15 reps forward and backward
  • High Knees – 1 minute
  • Hip Circles – 10 reps per side

Workout (30-40 minutes)

PCOD-Friendly Circuit (Repeat 3 times)

  • Goblet Squats – 12-15 reps
  • Incline Push-Ups – 10-12 reps
  • Donkey Kicks – 12 reps per leg
  • Side Plank (Knee Supported) – Hold for 20 seconds per side
  • Step-Ups (using a sturdy surface) – 10 reps per leg

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Pigeon Pose – Hold for 20 seconds per side
  • Seated Forward Fold – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Chest Opener Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds
  • Hamstring Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds per leg

Tips

  • Focus on proper form to prevent injuries.
  • Rest for 30-60 seconds between exercises.
  • Stay hydrated during and after workouts.
  • Gradually increase intensity as you feel stronger.
