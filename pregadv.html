Personalized Fitness Companion - Pregnancy Exercises

Tips

  • Always consult your doctor before starting a new exercise program.
  • Avoid exercises that require lying flat on your back after the first trimester.
  • Focus on gentle, low-impact exercises that enhance flexibility and strength.
  • Stay hydrated and listen to your body.

Beginner Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Neck Rolls – 5 reps clockwise and counterclockwise
  • Shoulder Rolls – 10 reps forward and backward
  • Side Leg Swings – 10 reps per leg
  • Marching in Place – 1 minute

Workout (20-30 minutes)

Pregnancy-Friendly Circuit (Repeat 2 times)

  • Pelvic Tilts – 10 slow reps
  • Seated Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 slow reps
  • Wall Push-Ups – 10 reps

Intermediate Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Arm Circles – 10 reps forward and backward
  • High Knees – 1 minute at a moderate pace
  • Torso Twists – 10 reps per side

Workout (20-30 minutes)

Intermediate Circuit (Repeat 3 times)

  • Sumo Squats – 12 reps
  • Modified Side Planks – Hold for 10 seconds per side
  • Seated Leg Lifts – 12 reps per leg
Stay active and healthy during pregnancy! Always listen to your body and adjust as needed.
