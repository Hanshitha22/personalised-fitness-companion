Personalized Fitness Companion - Thyroid Exercises (Intermediate Level)

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Dynamic Neck Stretches – 8 reps clockwise and counterclockwise
  • Arm Swings – 15 forward and backward
  • Standing Side Bends – 8 reps per side
  • High Knees – 1 minute

Workout (20-30 minutes)

Intermediate Thyroid Circuit (Repeat 3 times)

  • Warrior Pose I – Hold for 20-30 seconds per side
  • Plank Pose – Hold for 20-30 seconds
  • Standing Forward Fold with Arm Reach – 8 slow reps
  • Breathing Exercise (Alternate Nostril Breathing) – 2 minutes
  • Bridge Pose with Leg Lifts – 10 reps alternating legs

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Child’s Pose – Hold for 40 seconds
  • Sphinx Pose – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Seated Forward Fold – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Twisted Side Stretch – Hold for 15 seconds per side

Tips

  • Incorporate balance and strength exercises for better thyroid health.
  • Listen to your body and adjust intensity if needed.
  • Focus on deep breathing throughout the workout to stay relaxed.
  • Stay consistent to see long-term benefits for thyroid health.
