Advanced Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Jump Squats – 12-15 reps
  • Arm Swings – 20 reps
  • Butt Kicks – 1 minute
  • World's Greatest Stretch – 10 reps per side

Workout (40-50 minutes)

PCOD-Friendly Advanced Circuit (Repeat 3-4 times)

  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 12 reps per leg
  • Pike Push-Ups – 10-12 reps
  • Glute Bridges with Band – 15 reps
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps – 20 taps (10 per side)
  • Box Jumps or Jump Step-Ups – 12-15 reps

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Lizard Pose – Hold for 20 seconds per side
  • Butterfly Stretch – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 8 reps
  • Quad Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds per leg

Tips

  • Challenge yourself but avoid overtraining.
  • Focus on engaging core muscles for balance and stability.
  • Maintain a steady breathing rhythm during exercises.
  • Take 60-90 seconds of rest between circuits if needed.
