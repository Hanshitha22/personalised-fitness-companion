Beginner Level

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Seated Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 slow reps
  • Pelvic Tilts – 10 reps
  • Side Stretches – Hold for 10 seconds per side
  • Marching in Place – 1 minute

Workout (20-30 minutes)

PCOD-Friendly Circuit (Repeat 2 times)

  • Bodyweight Squats – 10-12 reps
  • Knee Push-Ups – 8-10 reps
  • Glute Bridges – 12-15 reps
  • Plank (Knees Supported) – Hold for 15-20 seconds
  • Wall Sits – Hold for 20 seconds

Cool-Down and Stretching (5-10 minutes)

  • Butterfly Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds
  • Child’s Pose – Hold for 30 seconds
  • Shoulder Stretch – Hold for 20 seconds per side
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 slow reps

Tips

  • Exercise regularly to help manage PCOD symptoms.
  • Keep movements controlled to reduce stress on joints.
  • Include light cardio exercises like walking.
  • Pair workouts with a balanced diet for better results.
